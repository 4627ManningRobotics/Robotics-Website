About Us

What are we all about?

A bit of our history: we are a team based in Calgary, Alberta, a city with a labour force heavily influenced by mechanics, engineers, and tradespeople. Manning Robotics Team 4627 was established in 2013 and started with just 10 students and 3 mentors. That same season, we won the Rookie All-Star Award at the Western Canada Regional Championship. Since then, we have grown into a group of 45 students with 12 teachers and volunteer mentors, and proven ourselves both nationally and internationally.
